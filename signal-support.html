Getting Started

Signal+ is designed to be simple. Log protein four times a day, spaced about four hours apart. The app prompts you when it's time to eat again — you just respond to it.

  • Tap LOG PROTEIN each time you consume 30g or more of protein
  • The arc dial shows how much time is left in your current window
  • Tap any logged bottle to edit the time if needed
  • The Apple Watch app lets you log without opening your phone

Notifications

Signal+ sends three types of reminders:

  • Morning nudge at 8 AM if you haven't logged your first protein yet
  • Feed Me reminders at 3.5, 4.0, and 4.5 hours after your last log
  • Optional hydration reminders at 10 AM, 2 PM, and 6 PM

All notifications can be managed in Settings inside the app, or in your iPhone's iOS Settings.

Apple Watch

The Signal+ Watch app shows your four bottles and a LOG PROTEIN button. Logs made on the watch sync to your iPhone automatically when both devices are nearby. If your phone isn't reachable, the log is queued and syncs when connectivity is restored.

Daily Reset

Signal+ resets automatically at midnight each day. Your previous day's logs are saved to your journal and a new day begins fresh.
