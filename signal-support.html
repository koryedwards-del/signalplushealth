GETTING STARTED

Signal+ is designed to be simple. Log protein four times a day, spaced about four hours apart. The app tracks your windows and prompts you when it's time to eat again.

  • Tap LOG PROTEIN each time you consume 30g or more of protein
  • The arc dial shows how much time is left in your current window
  • Tap any logged bottle to edit the time if needed

NOTIFICATIONS

Signal+ sends three types of reminders:

  • Morning nudge at 8 AM if you haven't logged your first protein yet
  • Feed Me reminders at 3.5, 4.0, and 4.5 hours after your last log
  • Optional hydration reminders at 10 AM, 2 PM, and 6 PM

All notifications can be managed in Settings inside the app.

DAILY RESET

Signal+ resets automatically at midnight each day. Your previous day's logs are saved to your journal and a new day begins fresh.

PROFILE SETTINGS

  • Age 65+ — tailors messaging for older adults managing sarcopenia risk
  • GLP-1 User — tailors messaging for users of Ozempic, Wegovy, Mounjaro, or Zepbound
