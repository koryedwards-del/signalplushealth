Privacy Policy

Effective Date: March 2026

Overview

Signal+ is a protein timing prompter built to help you maintain muscle through consistent mTOR activation. Your privacy is straightforward: we don't collect, share, or sell your personal data.

Data We Do Not Collect

Signal+ does not collect, transmit, or store any personal information on external servers. Specifically, we do not collect:

  • Your name, email address, or any identifying information
  • Location data
  • Health or fitness data beyond what you log in the app
  • Device identifiers or advertising IDs
  • Usage analytics or behavioral data

Data Stored on Your Device

All data you enter in Signal+ — your protein logs, timestamps, and settings — is stored locally on your iPhone and Apple Watch using Apple's SwiftData framework. This data never leaves your device unless you have iCloud enabled, in which case Apple syncs it to your personal iCloud account under Apple's privacy policy.

We have no access to this data at any time.

Notifications

Signal+ uses local notifications to remind you to log protein and hydrate. These notifications are generated entirely on your device. No notification content is transmitted to any server.

Apple Watch

The Signal+ Apple Watch app communicates with your iPhone using Apple's WatchConnectivity framework. This communication stays between your devices and does not involve any external servers or third parties.

Third Parties

Signal+ contains no third-party SDKs, analytics tools, advertising networks, or crash reporting services. There are no third parties with access to your data.

Children

Signal+ is not directed at children under 13 and does not knowingly collect information from children.

Changes to This Policy

If we update this privacy policy, we will update the effective date at the top of this page. Continued use of the app after any changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
