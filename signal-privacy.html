Privacy Policy

Effective Date: March 2026

Overview

Signal+ is a protein timing reminder built to help you maintain muscle through consistent mTOR activation. Your privacy is straightforward: we do not share or sell your personal data.

Data We Do Not Collect

Signal+ does not collect, transmit, or share any personal information with third parties. Specifically, we do not collect:

  • Your name or any identifying information beyond your account email
  • Location data
  • Health or fitness data beyond what you log in the app
  • Device identifiers or advertising IDs
  • Usage analytics or behavioral data

Data Stored in Your Account

All data you enter in Signal+ — your protein logs, timestamps, and settings — is stored securely in your account using Firebase, Google's cloud infrastructure. Data is tied to your account and is never shared or sold to any third party.

You may request deletion of your data at any time by contacting us at info@signalplushealth.com.

Notifications

Signal+ uses Android's native notification system to remind you to log protein and hydrate. Notifications are delivered through Firebase Cloud Messaging. No personal information is included in notification payloads.

All notifications can be managed in Settings inside the app.

Third Parties

Signal+ uses Firebase by Google for account management and data storage, and Firebase Cloud Messaging for notifications. No advertising networks, analytics tools, or other third-party SDKs are included in the app.

Children

Signal+ is not directed at children under 13 and does not knowingly collect information from children.

Changes to This Policy

If we update this privacy policy, we will update the effective date at the top of this page. Continued use of the app after any changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

Contact

Questions about this privacy policy? Contact us at info@signalplushealth.com
